Mad-libs story random-word filler

Type a mad-libs style story into the box below, and click the button to have the story filled with randomly selected words!



Usage

As you are typing your mad-lib story, indicate which words you would like to randomly replace by typing a part of speech enclosed by square brackets (e.g. "[noun]")

The finished story will be shown below. Words replaced will be colored red. Hovering over the words will show the definition.


Want an example story to try? Copy and paste this text:
Once there was a [noun] who went for a walk to the [noun]. On the way, it decided to [verb] very [adverb]- all the way there! What a [adjective] thing to do.
